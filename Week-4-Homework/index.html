Top 3 Builds

Fallout 76 Character Builds are essentially a way of life in Fallout. In order to be successfull in the game you need to distribute points into a S.P.E.C.I.A.L. category and select perk cards based on which attributes you chose. Perk Cards are individual cards with specific "perks" on them that enable you to have certain buffs or abilities
